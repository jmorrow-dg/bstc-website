# What is BSTC?

**BSTC (Bali Startup & Tech Community)** is the largest founder-led tech community in Bali and Southeast Asia. Founded in October 2023, we've grown to 2,500+ members across MeetUp and WhatsApp.

## Who's in the community?

Our members include ex-FAANG engineers, funded startup founders (pre-seed to Series C), VCs, angel investors, and senior operators. 40+ nationalities represented. The common thread: they're all building something real.

## What events do you run?

- **Core Networking Night** — Monthly, 40-80 people, free. Relaxed networking with optional open mic.
- **Founder Roundtable** — Monthly, max 20 people, $20K+ MRR founders only. Confidential, deep-dive discussion.
- **Builder Session** — Monthly, 30-60 people. Tactical talks and workshops.
- **How I Build with AI** — Our flagship series. Live AI builds by real practitioners.

## What makes BSTC different?

Three rules that define everything:

1. **Signal > Noise** — We focus on substance. No hype, no fluff.
2. **No Hard Selling** — Build relationships, not transactions. Pitching is not welcome.
3. **Builders First** — We prioritise people who are actually building things.

## How do I join?

1. **Join our WhatsApp** community for updates
2. **RSVP on MeetUp** for the next event
3. **Show up** and be yourself

It's that simple. See you in the room.
