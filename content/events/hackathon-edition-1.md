# BSTC Hackathon: Edition #1

The first BSTC hackathon. One day. Real teams. Real products. Real prizes.

## The Format

**09:00** Doors open. Coffee. Team formation for solo attendees.

**09:30** Kickoff. Theme revealed. Rules explained. Clock starts.

**09:30 to 17:00** Build. Seven and a half hours of focused building. Mentors float the room. Food and drinks provided.

**17:00** Tools down. Submissions locked.

**17:30** Demos. Each team gets 3 minutes to present what they built.

**18:00** Judging. Community vote + judge panel.

**18:30** Winners announced. Prizes awarded. Networking.

## Who Should Come

- Developers who want to ship something in a day
- Designers who want to work with real builders
- Founders who want to prototype an idea fast
- Anyone who wants to see what Bali's builder community can do under pressure

## Teams

- Come with a team (2 to 5 people) or form one at the event
- Solo attendees welcome: we'll help you find a team during the first 30 minutes
- Mixed skill teams encouraged: dev + design + business

## What You'll Need

- Laptop (fully charged, bring a charger)
- Your favourite tools and frameworks
- An idea (or willingness to join someone else's)
- Builder energy
