# The Room Where Builders Meet

Our flagship monthly networking night is the beating heart of BSTC. Every 3rd Thursday, 40-80 founders, engineers, investors, and operators gather at Yema Kitchen in Canggu for the highest-signal networking event in Bali.

## What to Expect

**6:00 PM**: Doors open. Grab a drink. Find your name tag.

**6:30 PM**: Welcome from the host. Quick housekeeping. "No hard selling. Signal over noise. Builder-first."

**6:40 PM**: Open mic intros. 60 seconds each. Name, what you're building, what you need. 10-15 people. Strict time limit.

**7:10 PM**: Open networking. The host floats, makes introductions, connects the right people. This is where the magic happens.

**8:00 PM**: Close. Thank the venue. Tease next month's event.

## The Rules

- **No hard selling.** No cornering people with pitches. No handing out business cards unless asked.
- **60-second intros.** Keep it tight. Respect the room's time.
- **Signal > Noise.** Every conversation should add value.
- **Host sets the tone.** The host floats, diffuses aggressive pitchers, and protects the vibe.

## Why People Keep Coming Back

This isn't a generic meetup. The people in this room are building real things: funded startups, AI products, SaaS platforms, dev tools. The conversations are substantive. The connections are genuine. The "no hard selling" rule makes all the difference.

**Free entry. Always.** RSVP on MeetUp to secure your spot.
